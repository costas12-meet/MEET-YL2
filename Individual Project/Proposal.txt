The app lets you take pictures, and using the accelerometer you can add effects. Then you can share the photos via e-mail to your contacts. Effects like a sword slash. Name: Slash n' Dash

Camera, accelerometer, touchscreen, phone contacts.

Buttons:
	- Share button
	- Take photograph button, or go to camera
	- A button for each effect
	- A button to apply effect

The user will open the app, see instructions, ok them away. They will then take a picture, see possible effects, apply them and save and share.
